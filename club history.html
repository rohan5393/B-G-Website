Club History

The John W. Hereford Boys and Girls Clubs of Huntington was founded in 1959. In 2015, The Clubs served 1 117 Cabell County children from Cabell County schools. Clubs are located in an independent location in Guyandotte and at Central City Elementary. Children aged 6 to 18 are served. The Clubs provide recreation, mentoring, homework help, and numerous field trips all at a low cost; membership is only $10 per year per child. A typical day includes: a free hot meal, a free snack, homework help, art, physical fitness, and interaction with a positive role model. Almost 100% of our school time population is comprised of Cabell County students.
