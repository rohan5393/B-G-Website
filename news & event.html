Partner of the month

The John W. Hereford Boys & Girls Clubs of Huntington, WV has served Huntington's youth since 1958. Our Clubs offer an exciting opportunity for your child to learn, play and grow. We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit after school program who serves children from 10 Cabell County schools. Homework help, physical fitness, art, music, and fields, are just some of the services we provide to our youth.
